Pomodoro is a technique aimed at improving productivity. It works this way:

Each session consists of a number of Pomodoros, each Pomodoro lasts 30 minutes, being 25 minutes of work plus a 5 minute break. A Pomodoro ends when its break time ends. A Pomodoro starts right after the previous one ends.
